iZwi is committed to keeping your personal information secure and within South African borders. All your data is stored on servers located in South Africa and is protected under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Who We Are

iZwi is a community alert platform that helps South African communities stay informed about local events, safety concerns, and important updates. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly in accordance with POPIA.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Your email address and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Your name and profile picture (optional)
  • Community Data: Information about your community membership and role
  • Alert Information: Content of alerts you post, including location data and descriptions
  • Usage Information: How you interact with our platform to improve our service

3. Why We Need Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide you with access to community alerts and updates
  • Allow you to post and manage alerts within your community
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • Communicate important updates about your account or community
  • Improve our service based on how it's used

Your personal information will never be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for commercial purposes. Your privacy is not for sale.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is stored on secure servers located within South Africa
  • We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information
  • Access to your data is strictly limited to authorized personnel
  • We regularly review and update our security measures
  • We comply with all POPIA requirements for data protection

5. Your POPIA Rights

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information
  • Object: Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only for as long as necessary to provide our service or as required by South African law. When you delete your account, we will permanently remove your personal information from our systems within 30 days.

7. Sharing Information

We only share your information within your community (as you choose when posting alerts) and with service providers who help us operate our platform, all of whom are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are located in South Africa.
